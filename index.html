05 · Questions, answered

The stuff people ask
before they sign up.

Does it work with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro or Jobber?
Yes — bookings drop straight onto your dispatch board with the right job type, customer info, and service area tag. We sit in front of the tools you already pay for, we don't replace them. Same with FieldEdge, Google Calendar, Outlook, HubSpot and most CRMs that accept webhooks.
How are emergencies handled?
You set the rules in onboarding — *no heat in winter, gas smell, sewage backup, panel fire, water damage,* whatever counts as urgent for your shop. The second TheMate hears one of those, it reassures the caller, captures the address and details, and texts your on-call tech with a 30-second summary — and can patch them straight through if you pick up.
What if a caller asks something complex it doesn't know?
It doesn't guess. If something's outside what you've trained — a one-off retrofit, a pricing edge case, anything technical it shouldn't speak to — TheMate captures the question, books a callback in your name, and texts you the summary so you call back informed. After a few weeks you'll see the recurring "didn't know" list and can decide what to add to the playbook.
Will customers know it's AI?
Most don't notice. TheMate sounds natural, handles interruptions, and knows when to stop talking. If a caller asks directly, TheMate answers honestly — and most people keep going because they're getting useful answers.
How is this different from a chatbot or IVR?
No menus, no "press 1." A homeowner says *"my basement is flooding"* — TheMate understands urgency, pages your on-call plumber, and tells the caller help's on the way. An IVR makes them dial 7 and listen to hold music.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from your discovery call. Day 1: 30-minute call where we collect your services, pricing, hours, escalation rules, calendar access. Day 2: we hand you a trained Mate to call and approve. Once you say yes, we flip the call-forwarding.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You forward your existing business line — for all calls, after-hours only, or as overflow when you can't pick up. Your number stays yours. Customers dial what they always have.
Can I change pricing or services later?
Anytime. Text or email us — *"diagnostic fee is now $89"*, *"we don't service zip 78250 anymore"*, *"add tankless installs at $2,800–4,500."* It's live within hours. No re-training call, no contract amendment.
