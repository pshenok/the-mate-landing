05 · Questions, answered

The stuff people ask
before they sign up.

Will customers know it's AI?
Most don't notice. TheMate speaks naturally, handles interruptions, and knows when to stop talking. If a caller asks directly, TheMate answers honestly — and most people keep talking anyway because they're getting useful answers.
How is this different from a chatbot or IVR?
No menus. No "press 1". TheMate has a conversation. If a caller says "my basement is flooding and I need someone now," TheMate understands urgency, pages you, and tells the caller help is on the way — something an IVR can't do.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from our first call. Day 1: 30-minute discovery. Day 2: we hand you a trained Mate to listen to and approve. Once you say yes, we flip the switch.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You forward your existing line — for all calls, or only when you can't pick up. Your number stays yours. Customers dial the same number they always have.
How are emergencies handled?
You set the rules during onboarding — e.g. "water damage, gas smell, or no heat in winter = emergency." When TheMate detects those keywords or patterns, it texts you immediately with the caller's name, number, and a 30-second summary, and can patch them straight through if you pick up.
Can I update TheMate when my business changes?
Anytime. Text us changes, or use the dashboard to edit pricing, hours, services, or emergency rules. Updates go live in minutes — no re-training call needed.
