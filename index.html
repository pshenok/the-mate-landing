FAQ

Common questions.

Will customers know it's AI?
Most won't notice. TheMate is trained to represent your brand naturally. If directly asked, it's transparent — we believe in honest AI.
How is TheMate different from a chatbot or IVR?
Chatbots read scripts. IVRs make you press buttons. TheMate holds real conversations because it's trained on your actual business — not a generic template.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from your onboarding call. We handle everything — you just review and approve before it goes live.
Do I need to change my phone number?
Never. Forward your existing number to TheMate during chosen hours. Your number stays yours.
How are emergencies handled?
We define triggers during onboarding. When detected, TheMate reassures the caller and fires an instant SMS alert to your team with full context.
