# Product Development Ideas for TheMate

### 1. Voice Persona Customization
- Allow users to choose specific tones (e.g., Professional, Friendly, Stoic) to match their local brand.
- Implement "Local Accent" optimization for specific regions in the US (Southern, East Coast, etc.).

### 2. Deep Integration with Vertical CRM
- Direct sync with ServiceTitan and Housecall Pro for real-time schedule booking without manual intervention.
- Automated follow-up SMS threads linked to the voice call history.

### 3. Multi-Modal Intake
- Ability for the AI to "view" images sent via MMS (e.g., a photo of a broken water heater) and provide a rough estimate based on trained pricing logic.

### 4. Advanced Lead Scoring (AI Audit Trail)
- Provide a "Confidence Score" for each booking, flagging calls where the human sounded hesitant for manual follow-up.
- Sentiment analysis of the caller to prioritize high-urgency/high-value jobs.

### 5. Multi-Tenant Analytics Dashboard
- Provide business owners with a heat-map of when their leads are calling (after-hours vs. peak).
- ROI calculator based on missed-calls captured by TheMate.

### 6. Automated Lead Quality Feedback Loop
- Implement a system where business owners can provide "thumbs up/down" on agentic summaries.
- Use this feedback to automatically refine the agent's prompt or local fine-tuning (R-LoRA) for better qualification accuracy.

### 7. Agentic Outbound follow-up
- Allow TheMate to not only receive calls but also make automated outbound follow-ups to stale leads or "warm" leads that haven't booked yet.
